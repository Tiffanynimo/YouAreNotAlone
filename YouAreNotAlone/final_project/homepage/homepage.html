About Us

We are a compassionate organization dedicated to supporting survivors of sexual assault. Our mission is to provide holistic care through therapy, medical aid, legal support, and education.

Our Mission

To create a safe, inclusive space for healing and justice.

Our Approach

Trauma-informed, survivor-centered, and accessible to all.

Support Services

Therapy

Confidential sessions with licensed trauma therapists.

Medical Aid

Access to emergency care, forensic exams, and follow-up treatment.

Legal Aid

Guidance from legal professionals to navigate reporting and justice systems.
